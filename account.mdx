## AI Credits

### How Credits Work

AI credits power content summarization:

**Content Types:**
- Articles/Newsletters: 1 credit each
- YouTube videos (< 30 min): 1 credit
- YouTube videos (30 min - 2 hrs): 2 credits
- YouTube videos (> 2 hrs): 3 credits

**Monthly Allocation:**
- All paid plans: 2000 credits/month
- Refreshes on the 1st
- No rollover to next month

### Managing Credits

**Monitor Usage:**
- Dashboard shows current balance
- Usage history by digest
- Projected monthly usage

**If You Run Out:**
- Wait for monthly refresh
- Purchase add-on credits
- Contact support for high-volume needs

**Credit-Free Actions:**
- Reading existing digests
- Managing settings
- Source configuration
