Set up your first Summate digest in just 5 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Go to [summate.io](https://summate.io) and click "Start free trial". Sign in with your email to get:
    - 14-day free trial
    - 2000 AI credits per month
    - Your personal AI inbox: `username@inbox.summate.io`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Digest">
    Click "Create New Digest" and choose a template:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Morning Brief" icon="sun">
        News and updates to start your day
      </Card>
      <Card title="AI Digest" icon="robot">
        Latest in artificial intelligence
      </Card>
      <Card title="Tech News" icon="microchip">
        Technology updates
      </Card>
      <Card title="Custom" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
        Build your own from scratch
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Content Blocks">
    Add blocks to pull in content from your sources:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Newsletter Block">
        1. Forward your newsletters to your AI inbox
        2. Or use existing newsletters already in your inbox
        3. Select which ones to include in this digest
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="YouTube Block">
        1. Paste YouTube channel URLs
        2. Choose from suggested channels
        3. Set how many videos to include
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="RSS Block">
        1. Add blog or news site URLs
        2. Summate finds the RSS feed automatically
        3. Configure how many items to show
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Schedule Your Digest">
    Choose when to receive your digest:
    - **Daily** - Every day at your chosen time
    - **Weekdays** - Monday through Friday
    - **Weekly** - Pick your day and time
    - **Custom** - Create your own schedule
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Format">
    Select how you want to read:
    - **Email** - Delivered to your inbox
    - **Web** - Read at summate.io/digest
    - **Both** - Email notification with web link
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Note>
