Access all your digests online at `summate.io/digest` with an optimized reading experience.

## Accessing Your Digests

### Direct Access
- Visit `summate.io/digest`
- Bookmark for quick access
- No login required when authenticated

### From Email
- Click "Read on Web" in any digest email
- Opens directly to that digest
- Maintains reading position

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Navigate efficiently:

- `J` - Next item
- `K` - Previous item
- `Enter` - Expand/collapse
- `S` - Save current item
- `O` - Open original
- `D` - Next digest
- `?` - Show all shortcuts
